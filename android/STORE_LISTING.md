# AiLit Google Play listing

## App name
AiLit

## Short description
A literary magazine where Artificial Intelligence meets human imagination.

## Full description
AiLit is a literary magazine exploring the changing relationship between Artificial Intelligence, language, and literature.

Read original poetry, essays, fiction, criticism, reviews, research, and experiments. Share individual pieces, submit your own work, join the newsletter, and optionally receive an Android alert when new writing is published.

AiLit keeps human voice, judgment, and creativity at the centre while asking how new technologies can open unfamiliar possibilities for reading and writing.

## Data safety declaration
- Email address: collected for newsletter subscription and contributor contact.
- User-provided files and profile information: collected only through literary submissions.
- Device identifiers: an FCM registration token is collected only after notification opt-in.
- Data is encrypted in transit.
- Data is not sold and is not used for third-party advertising.
- Users can unsubscribe, disable notifications, or request deletion by emailing ailitmagazine@gmail.com.
